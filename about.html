Our Journey

History About The Hospital

St. Theresa Hospital was established in 1974 as a response to the pressing healthcare needs of Nkoranza and its surrounding communities. Founded on the principles of the Catholic Church compassion, dignity, and service the hospital began with a modest facility and a courageous vision: to provide quality healthcare to all, regardless of their ability to pay.

In its early years, the hospital operated with limited resources but boundless dedication. Local community leaders, health professionals, and religious organizations came together to establish this beacon of hope. Over the decades, through strategic expansion, investment in modern equipment, and recruitment of talented medical professionals, St. Theresa has grown into one of the most trusted healthcare institutions in the region.

Key Milestones:

From our humble beginnings with a single operating theater and 20 beds, we have expanded to include specialized departments such as neurosurgery, renal dialysis, radiology, mental health services, and a comprehensive maternity unit. Each expansion has been driven by community needs and our commitment to advancing healthcare quality.

Our journey has been marked by resilience, innovation, and an unwavering dedication to the people we serve. Today, we continue to write our story‚Äîone patient, one family, one community at a time.
