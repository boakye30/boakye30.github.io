Our Story

A Legacy of Healing,
A Future of Innovation

St. Theresa Hospital has been a cornerstone of healthcare in the Bono East Region for over five decades. Founded on the values of the Catholic Church, we combine faith-driven compassion with modern medical excellence.

Our mission is simple: to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to every patient who walks through our doors — regardless of their background or means.
